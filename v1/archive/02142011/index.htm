february fourteenth, two thousand eleven







dearest joy,

happy valentine's day sweetheart! i'm sorry that i didn't come up with an entirely different way of letting you know how much i care about you, but traditions can be fun too. once again, a visit is imminent and i am incredibly excited to show you my new house. its been a long year, and i still don't quite know how i feel about this place. i am trying to make the best of it, but i know it will feel a lot more like home as soon as you arrive.

i still remember laying in bed with you in your apartment on i street, staring at the ceiling and giggling while harriet and maynard played in the kitchen. i felt so close to you then. and while the distance can be challenging at times, 3,000 miles really hasn't changed anything.

i love you joy marie(?) gardner.

i'll see you soon.
