back in HS i made a few friends working at Souplantation. they were years older and they introduced me to 'indie music' (among other things).

I thought i was pretty cool listening to bands that no one else at Etiwanda had ever heard of, but the vast majority of it was derivitive pop-punk with power chords in 4
4 time.

this song in particular caught Aaron's attention though. initially i think because of the odd time signature. He patiently tried to teach me not only what it was ( 7
8  ? 7
7  ? ), but how to work out what it was, more than once. obviously neither stuck with me.

i do still remember him sitting in the passenger seat of my parked car though, tapping his knee to the beat with his eyes closed, his shaggy hair shaking from side to side while he smiled.
